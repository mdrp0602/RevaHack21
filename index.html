Onocology Departmet

The Prime Hostel Cancer Center is designated by the National Cancer Institute as a comprehensive cancer center. This means the clinic's renowned physicians, researchers and scientists carry out team-based, patient-centered research to develop the latest technologies and treatments to address unmet patient needs. As a result, people who come to the clinic for cancer care have access to hundreds of clinical trials in all phases. Mayo Clinic researchers share what they learn with other members of the National Comprehensive Cancer Network, a not-for-profit alliance of leading cancer centers dedicated to improving the quality, effectiveness and efficiency of care for cancer patients.

Orthology Department

Orthology offers personalized, non-invasive orthopedic physical therapy, sports medicine and chiropractic care that focuses on the root cause of injuries to help patients get better faster and live a more active and pain-free life. Orthology offers a best-in-class, hands-on treatment approach delivered by leading physical therapists and chiropractors in cutting-edge clinics throughout Minneapolis, New York City and Long Island. According to Focus on Therapeutics Outcomes data, Orthology patients show a return to function in half the number of visits as patients from other clinics, with most patients completing treatment in four to eight visits. Orthology accepts all major insurance plans, with same and next-day appointments available.
